  1. Pearcey Kernel Basis:
    Beyond Second Order Differential Equations

    There is a known connection between orthogonal polynomials, second order Sturm-Liouville differential equations, random matrices, and orthogonal projectors in \( L_2 \). This post is about an extension of this topic. We find a basis of the Pearcey Kernel introduced by Brezin and Hikami. To this end we have to break away from standard theory of second order differential equations and orthogonal polynomials to third order equations and biorthogonal bases.
